01 // CURRENT INVESTIGATION

Classical Rocket Dynamics

I am deriving the generalized Tsiolkovsky equation for non-inertial reference frames. Unlike standard textbook approximations, my model focuses on incorporating atmospheric density variations $\rho(h)$ and drag coefficients $C_D$ as dynamic variables rather than constants. This allows for higher precision in predicting apogee for sub-orbital trajectories.
